# PayChangu Tools (12)

MCP tools for PayChangu payment operations.

## Payment & Transactions (4 tools)

### Initiate Payment

Create a hosted checkout payment.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Create a PayChangu payment for 1000 MWK"*
- *"Initiate a checkout for customer john@example.com for 5000 MWK"*

### Verify Transaction

Check if a transaction was successful.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Verify PayChangu transaction TX_12345"*
- *"Check if payment order-456 was successful"*

### Initiate Direct Charge

Charge a customer directly.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Process a direct charge of 2000 MWK"*

### Get Transaction Details

Get full transaction information.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Get details for transaction TX_12345"*

## Bank Transfers (2 tools)

### Process Bank Transfer

Send money to a bank account.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Transfer 10000 MWK to bank account 123456789 at National Bank"*

### Get Supported Banks

List available banks.

**Example prompts:**
- *"What banks does PayChangu support?"*
- *"Show me supported banks for MWK"*

## Mobile Money & Payouts (6 tools)

### Get Mobile Operators

List available mobile money operators.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Show me PayChangu mobile operators"*
- *"What mobile money providers are available?"*

### Mobile Money Payout

Send money via mobile money.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Send 5000 MWK to 265991234567 via mobile money"*
- *"Process a mobile payout to Airtel number 265888123456"*

### Bank Payout

Send money to a bank account.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Send 20000 MWK to bank account 987654321"*

### Get Payout Details

Check payout status.

**Example prompts:**
- *"What's the status of payout payout-123?"*

### List All Payouts

Get all your payouts.

**Example prompts:**
- *"Show me all my PayChangu payouts"*
- *"List recent payouts"*
